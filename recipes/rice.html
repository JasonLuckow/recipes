Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain white rice
  • 5 cups of water
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 Tablespoon caldo de pollo

Steps

  1. Wash rice
  2. Blend water, tomatoes, garlic, onion, and caldo de pollo
  3. Put rice in pre heated, oil covered, deep dish pan
  4. Let fry for 5-10 minutes, occasionally stirring
  5. Add rice mix
  6. Cover and let cook for about 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
