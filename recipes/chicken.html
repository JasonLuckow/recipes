Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • Chicken fajita seasoning
  • Paper towels
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Completely dry chicken
  2. Apply seasoning, pat each side with paper towel
  3. Pre heat skillet
  4. Add olive oil
  5. Add chicken serving side down
  6. Cook for 3-4 minutes
  7. Add butter
  8. Flip chicken, and cook for 3-4 minutes
  9. Flip chicken, and cook for 3-4 minutes
  10. Flip chicken, and cook for 3-4 minutes
  11. Remove, and top with parsley
  12. Rest for 3-4 minutes
  13. Cut and serve
